Food

On a trip. also very important is FOOD. Once you eat a great breakfast, you can enjoy the places you want to visit. As for lunch, it's the perfect opportunity to try their traditional food... at least once in a lifetime ;) (because sometimes it's expensive). That's why, from my own experience I used to try something new a few times, and then eat from supermarkets or fast-food.

TIPS:

⁜ Always search for a supermarket or a mall near you.
⁜ If you consider that the following day will be full, you should not leave the hotel room without water and something sweet.

Hotel

To fully enjoy your trip, rest it crucial!
An ideal hotel means good location, low price, breakfast included, a lot of facilities and good reviews also. I, personally, choose first after location: either in central zone, either near metro or bus station. From my own experience, you can't always trust the reviews, that's why the location is the most accurate.
And considering the money, I choose to pay less for a hotel (decent, please), because I believe you only stay the nights in the hotel, when you're supposed to sleep.

Transport
The easiest and best way to fall in love with the city is to take their bus or metro. You get to be one of them... what could you ask more? That's why it's important to find the best route at the most convenient price and from all of my trips I've taken, I learned that one-way tickets are not effective at all. Even though you may think you don't need more tickets, you never know when you'll get in a hurry or if you get on the wrong bus.. so trust me, travel cards are the best!

TIPS:

⁜ Download offline maps before your trip. You never know when you run out of internet or signal on the phone.
⁜ For metro, it's very good to have a map with all of the routes.
⁜ Even though metro seems the fastest way, use also the bus and if you have plenty of time, visit the city while taking the bus from one end to the other.
⁜ To find the best prices for flight tickets, you should book them 6 weeks in advance.
⁜ If you want to make your trip more interesting, you should take the train or the bus between 2 close countries. For example, between London and Paris there are two ways: 2.5 hours with the train under the Channel Tunnel or with the bus (I like a lot FlixBus). The last one makes you feel like you're on the Titanic (because you'll also take the ferry).
⁜ Buy airline tickets directly from their official websites.
